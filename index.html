Biography

I am currently pursuing my B.E. degree from School of Computer and Information Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University, China. My research interests cover a broad range of data mining, machine learning, and brain computer interface. As a highly motivated student, I have participated in several research projects in the area of time series data mining and machine learning during my undergraduate studies.
